## How to Disable or Enable the Windows Key

### To **disable** the Windows key:

1. Click **Start**, click **Run**, type `regedt32`, and then click **OK**.
2. In the Registry Editor, navigate to:  
   `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Keyboard Layout`
3. On the **Edit** menu, click **Add Value**:
   - Name: `Scancode Map`
   - Data Type: `REG_BINARY`
   - Data:  
     ```
     00000000 00000000 03000000 00005BE0 00005CE0 00000000
     ```
4. Click **OK**.
5. Close the Registry Editor and **restart** your computer.

---

### To **enable** the Windows key (undo the change):

1. Click **Start**, click **Run**, type `regedt32`, and then click **OK**.
2. Navigate to:  
   `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Keyboard Layout`
3. Right-click the `Scancode Map` registry entry and click **Delete**.
4. Confirm by clicking **Yes**.
5. Close the Registry Editor and **restart** your computer.
